# The Edwin Group - Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategy
The Edwin Group embraces a Unified Brand Voice across all sub-groups. The brand language should be supportive, educational, clear, and modern.

## 2. Color Palette
The color system emphasizes approachability and professionalism.
- **Primary Backgrounds:** Warm, comforting tones (e.g., Soft Beige `#F5F2F0`, Warm Sand `#EAE1DA`).
- **Accent Tones:** Earthy and vibrant tones (e.g., Terracotta `#D96A4D`, Forest Green `#2A5A4A`, Mustard Yellow `#E4A93C`).

## 3. Typography
Typography creates a balance of traditional authority and modern accessibility.
- **Headings:** Bold serifs or geometric sans-serifs (e.g., *Playfair Display* or *Outfit* / *Poppins*).
- **Body Copy:** Clean, highly legible sans-serifs (e.g., *Inter* or *Roboto*).

## 4. UI/UX Principles
- **Grid System:** Modular, card-based grid system for organizing diverse content domains.
- **Micro-interactions:** Smooth hover states, seamless page transitions, parallax scrolling.
- **Assets:** Custom 3D models featuring soft, tactile, abstract educational shapes with subtle hover animations.
- **Responsiveness:** Full responsive translation of heavy 3D elements and typography from desktop to mobile.
