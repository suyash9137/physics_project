Enterprise Security & Scale

Our AI-powered Random-Sample Proctoring System is built to scale across global institutions without breaking bandwidth budgets.

🔒

Military-Grade Encryption

All captured media chunks are AES-256 encrypted in transit and at rest. Data is strictly isolated per institutional tenant.

📉

Bandwidth Optimization

By capturing 5-second random clips rather than continuous streaming, RSPS caps data uploads to just 15-20MB per student per hour—ideal for low-bandwidth regions.

🗑️

Automated Compliance

To adhere to GDPR and global privacy standards, our security pipelines automatically wipe all proctoring media records after 30 days.

🤖

AI-Powered Oversight

Librosa Voice Activity Detection and MediaPipe Head Pose Estimation run intelligently to identify anomalous behavior without human intervention.
